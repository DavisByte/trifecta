BMI Calculator



Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple method used to assess whether an individual has a healthy body weight for a given height. It is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters. BMI provides a useful estimate for most people. A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is generally considered normal.
